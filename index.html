Hello, World.

My name is Justin Luthy, and I'm a professional soccer player, and developer. After graduating from Boston College in 2013, I moved to Portland to play soccer. Suddenly I found myself with hours of free time. At the encouragement of some friends, I decided to teach myself to code.

I have always been interested in startups, apps, and the web so it was exciting to start to learn about how these products and companies are built. Over the past three years I've learned a lot about topics ranging from design and usability, to fundamentals of CS and building large software products, and I'm looking forward to learning even more.
